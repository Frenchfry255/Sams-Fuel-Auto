Frequently Asked Questions

Home FAQ

Do you specialize in any particular types of vehicles?

We work on all types of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, trucks/worktrucks, SUVs, and hybrids.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open from 6:30am to 6:30pm, Monday through Friday, and from 9am to 6pm on the weekend.

How do I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment by using our online appointment form on our website. If you aren't able to use the website than you may call as well.

Do you offer any kind of warranty?

Yes, we offer a 12-month or 12,000-mile warranty on all of our repair services. This warranty covers any defects in workmanship or materials.

How long does it typically take to complete a repair?

The time it takes to complete a repair varies depending on the type of repair and the availability of parts, but we always strive to complete repairs as quickly as possible without sacrificing quality.

Can I get a quote before I bring in my vehicle?

Yes, we can provide you with a quote for the cost of a repair before you bring in your vehicle. Just give us a call or fill out our online contact form.

How often should I get my oil changed?

The recommended frequency for oil changes varies depending on the make and model of your vehicle, but a general rule of thumb is every 3,000 to 5,000 miles or every three to six months.

How often should I get my brakes checked?

We recommend having your brakes checked at least once a year or whenever you hear unusual noises or experience any other issues with your brakes.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for senior citizens and military personnel. We also offer periodic specials and promotions, so check our website or follow us on social media for the latest deals.

Can I bring my own parts for you to install?

We generally prefer to use our own parts, as we can guarantee the quality and compatibility. However, we may be able to install your own parts in some cases. Contact us for more information.

Do you offer a shuttle service or loaner cars?

No, we do not offer a shuttle service or loaner cars for our customers to use while their vehicle is being serviced. If you need a loaner car you will have to use a car rental service

Can I wait while my vehicle is being serviced?

Yes, you are welcome to wait in our comfortable waiting area while your vehicle is being serviced.

What if I have a warranty on my vehicle?

We can work on vehicles that are still under warranty, as long as the repairs are not covered by the warranty. We can also provide documentation of the repairs that we perform.

How do I know when it's time to replace my tires?

You should replace your tires when the tread depth is worn down to 2/32 of an inch or less, or if you see any other signs of wear or damage.

Do you offer any roadside assistance?

No, we do not offer roadside assistance. However, we can recommend a reliable roadside assistance service if you need one.

Can you service my vehicle if it's not running?

Yes, we can service your vehicle even if it's not running. We offer towing services to bring your vehicle to our shop if necessary.

What if I have a question that's not listed here?

Feel free to contact us by phone, email, or through our website's contact form with any additional questions you may have. We're always happy to help!

We know that honesty and transparency, coupled with reliable and friendly customer service, is what really builds customer trust.

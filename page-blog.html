Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services, including:

  • Oil changes
  • Tire rotation and balancing
  • Brake repair and replacement
  • Suspension repair
  • Engine tune-ups
  • Diagnostic testing

What are your hours of operation?

We are open from 8am to 6pm, Monday through Friday, and from 9am to 3pm on Saturdays. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you offer any kind of warranty?

Yes, we offer a 12-month or 12,000-mile warranty on all of our repair services. This warranty covers any defects in workmanship or materials.

How do I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment by calling us at (123) 456-7890 or by using our online appointment form on our website.

Do you offer any kind of financing?

Yes, we offer financing options through our partner, XYZ Financing. Contact us for more information.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for senior citizens and military personnel. We also offer periodic specials and promotions, so check our website or follow us on social media for the latest deals.

Can I bring my own parts for you to install?

We generally prefer to use our own parts, as we can guarantee the quality and compatibility. However, we may be able to install your own parts in some cases. Contact us for more information.

We know that honesty and transparency, coupled with reliable and friendly customer service, is what really builds customer trust.
